            Basic Conference Packages
        
            The basic conference package is suitable for smaller-scale events 
            or conferences where the focus is primarily on informative 
            sessions and networking. It includes :

            1. Access to the conference hall for a full day
            2. Basic audiovisual equipment (projector, screen, microphone)
            3. Wi-Fi connectivity
            3. Free parking for a fair amount of attendee
            4. Water and refreshments during breaks

            Start from IDR Rp.12.500.000++

          Premium Conference Packages
      
    The premium conference package is ideal for larger-scale conferences,
    corporate events, or high-profile gatherings that require top-notch 
    audiovisual equipment and a more sophisticated setup. It includes :

    1. Access to the conference hall for a full day
    2. High-quality audiovisual equipment 
    3. Dedicated technical support
    4. Wi-Fi connectivity
    5. Customized branding options (logo projection, banners)
    6. Free parking for a larger amount of attendee
    7. Water, refreshments, and snacks during breaks

    Start from IDR Rp.25.000.000++
