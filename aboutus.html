            Excellence

Have been standing for over 10 years, JCC have witnessed and hosted more than 100 national events and 50 International Event Spanning across an expansive facility, our state-of-the-art infrastructure boasts versatile spaces designed to accommodate conferences, conventions, and gatherings of any scale. With advanced audiovisual equipment, flexible seating arrangements, and impeccable amenities, we ensure a seamless experience for attendees.

At the centerpiece of Jockey Conference Center lies our grand conference hall, spanning an impressive 10,000 square feet. With its high ceilings and customizable layout, the hall accommodates up to 500 guests. Adjacent breakout rooms and lounges offer additional space for workshops, networking sessions, and private meetings. Our commitment to excellence ensures that every event hosted at Jockey Conference Center surpasses expectations and leaves a lasting impression.
